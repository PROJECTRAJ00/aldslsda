About the firm

About us

A proprietorship practice bringing accounting, taxation and corporate-law perspective together.

Pincha and Associates is a proprietorship firm of Chartered Accountants headquartered in Bikaner, Rajasthan, with offices in Jaipur, Jodhpur, Kota, Sri Ganganagar, Surat and Varanasi. The firm serves proprietors, partnership firms, companies and individuals through every stage of the financial year.

Led by a proprietor qualified as a Chartered Accountant, Company Secretary and in Law, the practice looks at a client's accounting, taxation and corporate-legal matters together rather than in isolation. Confidentiality and professional independence guide every engagement.
